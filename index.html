Digital Footprint

what is a Digital Footprint?

The digital footprint is the trace you leave when browsing the internet

Tips for taking care of your digital footprint

Be Careful About what you share,Never share personal information such as your name, address, email address, phone number, or what school you attend with people you do not know personally and be smart about sites you visit, never click on sites that seem questionable. Only use information from reputable sites.

Drawbacks

Some people may be concerned about how much information is collected through footprint and how it is used on the other hand they can impersonate you by creating fake profiles based on photographs that you have uploaded to websites and social networks.
